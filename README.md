# Proptech Operations Case Study

## Overview

This case study presents a practical proptech operations workflow for real estate and property management teams.

It combines rental market analytics, CRM lead scoring, tenant inquiry automation, and property operations KPI tracking into one end-to-end business workflow.

The goal is to show how data workflows and AI-assisted automation can improve real estate operations without replacing human decision-making.

## Business Problem

Real estate teams often manage several operational workflows manually:

- Rental market benchmarking
- Buyer and tenant lead prioritisation
- Tenant enquiry triage
- Maintenance and complaint routing
- Leasing funnel tracking
- Underperforming listing detection
- Property manager workload monitoring

These workflows are often handled across disconnected tools such as spreadsheets, emails, CRM systems, listing platforms, and property management software.

This creates several operational problems:

- Slow response time
- Missed high-intent leads
- Inconsistent tenant communication
- Limited visibility into listing performance
- Manual reporting workload
- Delayed detection of underperforming listings
- Difficulty prioritising urgent issues

## Proposed Solution

This case study proposes an integrated real estate operations intelligence workflow.

The workflow includes four core modules:

1. Sydney Rental Market Dashboard
2. Real Estate CRM Lead Scoring Dashboard
3. Tenant Inquiry Automation Assistant
4. Property Operations KPI Dashboard

Together, these modules support the full operational flow from market analysis to lead handling, tenant communication, and performance monitoring.
