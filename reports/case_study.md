\# Case Study: Improving Real Estate Operations with Data Workflows and AI-assisted Automation



\## 1. Executive Summary



Real estate and property management teams handle large volumes of operational information every day, including rental market data, buyer and tenant enquiries, tenant messages, listing performance, maintenance issues, and leasing outcomes.



Many of these workflows are still handled manually across emails, spreadsheets, CRM systems, and property management platforms.



This case study proposes a practical proptech operations workflow that uses data dashboards and AI-assisted automation to support four key areas:



1\. Rental market benchmarking

2\. CRM lead prioritisation

3\. Tenant inquiry triage

4\. Property operations KPI tracking



The goal is not to replace property managers or agents, but to reduce repetitive manual work, improve visibility, and support faster decision-making.
